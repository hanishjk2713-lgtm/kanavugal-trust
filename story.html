Real Impact Stories

Priya — Coimbatore

Priya almost stopped her education due to financial struggles, but support from KANAVUGAL TRUST helped her continue schooling.

With educational materials and mentorship support, Priya completed her academic year successfully and now dreams of becoming a teacher to help other children like her.

Arun — Chennai

Arun’s family faced severe hardship during unemployment. Community food support helped them regain stability.

Regular welfare assistance allowed Arun to focus on skill training programs. Today he works in a small company and supports his family independently.

Meena — Madurai

Meena joined youth empowerment workshops that improved her confidence and communication skills.

After training sessions, Meena started leading community activities and inspiring other young women to pursue education.

Rahul — Trichy

Rahul received school supplies through donation programs, allowing him to return to school.

His academic performance improved drastically and he now aims to become an engineer, proving small help creates big change.

Selvi — Erode

Healthcare awareness programs helped Selvi’s family access medical support during difficult times.

The initiative improved health conditions and encouraged community participation in wellness programs.

Kumar — Salem

Volunteer-led mentoring helped Kumar develop technical skills and confidence for employment.

Today Kumar actively volunteers with the trust, helping other students build career paths and opportunities.
